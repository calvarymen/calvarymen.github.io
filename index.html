Do you know how to share your faith with other people? What if there was a way to make evangelism easy? Maybe even fun? Would you do it?

  • Session 1: How to Have a Heart for the Lost
  • Session 2: What is the Gospel?
  • Session 3: How to Navigate Conversations
  • Session 4: How to Defend Your Faith
  • Session 5: Q&A

Let's be equipped with a boldness and love for the lost! Continental breakfast and lunch will be provided.
